    • Short Bio

    Currently, Sheng Xu is a Lecture in the College of Information Science and Technology at the Nanjing Forestry University. Dr. Xu holds a Ph.D. in Digital Image Systems from the University of Calgary, and a B.Eng in the Computer Science and Technology from the Nanjing Forestry University.

    Interests

    Mobile Mapping, Vegetation Mapping and Computer Vision
